Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member of Alum Forge?

To become a member, simply click on the "Register Now" button on our homepage and fill out the registration form. You'll need to provide your graduation year, degree program, and current contact information. Membership is free for all Thapar University alumni.

What benefits do I get as a member?

As a member, you get access to our exclusive alumni directory, job board, networking events, mentorship opportunities, and professional development workshops. You also receive regular updates about university news and alumni achievements.

How can I update my contact information?

You can update your contact information by logging into your member account on our website, or by contacting our membership services team at membership@alumforge.com. We encourage you to keep your information current to stay connected with the community.

Can I volunteer for Alum Forge events?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers for our events and activities. You can express your interest by contacting our events team at events@alumforge.com or by filling out the volunteer form available on our website.

How do I get in touch with other alumni?

You can search our alumni directory by graduation year, degree program, location, or industry. You can also join our social media groups and attend networking events to connect with fellow alumni in person.
