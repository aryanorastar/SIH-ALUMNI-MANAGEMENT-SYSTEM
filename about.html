About Alum Forge

Connecting generations of Thapar University alumni to create lasting impact and meaningful relationships.

Our Mission

The Thapar alumni network is a global community of 25,000+ accomplished graduates, fostering excellence and connection through mentorship, events, and professional support. Alumni hold top roles worldwide as CEOs, engineers, scientists, and leaders, exemplifying Thapar’s legacy in academia and industry.

Membership offers ongoing access to valuable professional connections and opportunities, empowering alumni to contribute to Thapar Institute’s growth and societal impact—advancing Thapar’s reputation for excellence in every sphere.

Our Core Values

Collaboration

We believe in the power of working together to achieve greater outcomes than any individual could accomplish alone.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, maintaining the high standards that Thapar University is known for.

Community

We foster a sense of belonging and mutual support that extends beyond graduation and throughout our careers.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and technologies to continuously improve how we connect and serve our alumni community.
